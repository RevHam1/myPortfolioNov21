The Interview App

The purpose of this section is to show case a brief portion of the application functionality of this portfolio with a data base; while at the same time answering some basic questions about Django.

The Django Interview App

Overview:

The interview app will be in a question-and-answer type format with hopes to prepare the individual for a Django framework interview. Although away question can be asked or answered the focus of this app will be a Django Questionnaire Interview. This app will have a backend, with one-to-many relationship, utilizing CRUD functionalities.
